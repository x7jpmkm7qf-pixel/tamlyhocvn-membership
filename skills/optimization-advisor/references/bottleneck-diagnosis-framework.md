# Bottleneck Diagnosis Framework

## Overview

Systematic approach to identify where customers drop off in your business funnel and what actions to take.

## Funnel Stages

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Definition**: People see your content but don't engage

**Key Metrics**:
- Impressions/Reach
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Profile visits

**Benchmarks**:
- Social media engagement: 1-3%
- Email open rate: 20-30%
- Ad CTR: 0.5-2%

**Diagnostic Questions**:
- Is content reaching target audience?
- Does content stop the scroll?
- Is posting frequency consistent?
- Are you using platform best practices?

### Stage 2: Traffic
**Definition**: People see content but don't click through

**Key Metrics**:
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Link clicks vs impressions
- Traffic sources

**Benchmarks**:
- Social media CTR: 2-3%
- Email CTR: 2-5%
- Ad CTR to landing page: 1-3%

**Diagnostic Questions**:
- Is the headline compelling?
- Is the call-to-action clear?
- Does the offer match audience interest?
- Is there friction in clicking (too many steps)?

### Stage 3: Conversion
**Definition**: People visit but don't purchase

**Key Metrics**:
- Landing page conversion rate
- Add-to-cart rate
- Checkout completion rate

**Benchmarks**:
- Landing page conversion: 2-5%
- Cart abandonment: 60-80%
- Overall visitor-to-buyer: 1-3%

**Diagnostic Questions**:
- Is the offer clear and compelling?
- Is pricing competitive or justified?
- Are there enough trust signals?
- Is checkout process simple?
- Is page load speed acceptable?

### Stage 4: Retention
**Definition**: People buy once but don't return

**Key Metrics**:
- Repeat purchase rate
- Customer lifetime value
- Refund/churn rate

**Benchmarks**:
- Repeat purchase rate: 20-40%
- Refund rate: <5%
- Monthly churn: <10%

**Diagnostic Questions**:
- Is product quality meeting expectations?
- Is post-purchase communication working?
- Are there upsell/cross-sell opportunities?
- Is customer support responsive?

## Decision Tree: If → Then

### Awareness Problems

**If engagement rate < 1%**:
- **Then**: Review content quality and relevance
- **Action**: Test 5 different hook formulas, analyze top performers in niche

**If reach declining week-over-week**:
- **Then**: Algorithm penalizing your content
- **Action**: Increase posting consistency, boost engagement bait (questions, polls)

**If impressions high but engagement low**:
- **Then**: Content not resonating with audience
- **Action**: Survey audience, analyze competitor content, test new formats

### Traffic Problems

**If CTR < 2%**:
- **Then**: Headline/CTA needs improvement
- **Action**: A/B test 3 headline formulas, make CTA benefit-specific

**If clicks happen but bounce rate > 70%**:
- **Then**: Mismatch between promise and landing page
- **Action**: Align landing page headline with source content

**If traffic sources unbalanced (1 source = 80%+)**:
- **Then**: High dependency risk
- **Action**: Diversify traffic channels, test new platforms

### Conversion Problems

**If landing page conversion < 2%**:
- **Then**: Offer, trust, or clarity issue
- **Action**: Add social proof, simplify offer, improve headline, check page speed

**If cart abandonment > 80%**:
- **Then**: Checkout friction or trust issue
- **Action**: Reduce form fields, add trust badges, enable guest checkout

**If add-to-cart rate good but purchase low**:
- **Then**: Price shock or checkout complexity
- **Action**: Show total price earlier, simplify payment options

**If mobile conversion << desktop**:
- **Then**: Mobile experience broken
- **Action**: Test mobile checkout flow, optimize for thumb navigation

### Retention Problems

**If refund rate > 5%**:
- **Then**: Product quality or expectation mismatch
- **Action**: Improve product description accuracy, add demo/preview

**If repeat purchase rate < 10%**:
- **Then**: No post-purchase engagement
- **Action**: Implement email sequence, create loyalty program, add upsells

**If customer lifetime value declining**:
- **Then**: Losing customers too quickly
- **Action**: Survey churned customers, improve onboarding, add value touchpoints

## Red Flag Indicators

| Metric | Red Flag Threshold | Severity | Immediate Action |
|--------|-------------------|----------|------------------|
| Engagement Rate | < 0.5% | Critical | Content audit + format test |
| CTR | < 1% | High | Headline rewrite |
| Landing Page Conversion | < 1% | Critical | Full page redesign |
| Cart Abandonment | > 85% | High | Checkout simplification |
| Refund Rate | > 10% | Critical | Product quality review |
| Repeat Purchase | < 5% | High | Post-purchase sequence |
| Email Open Rate | < 15% | Medium | Subject line testing |
| Page Load Time | > 3 seconds | High | Speed optimization |

## Quick Wins vs Strategic Fixes

### Quick Wins (Implement This Week)
- **Headlines**: Test new headline formulas
- **CTAs**: Make calls-to-action benefit-specific
- **Social Proof**: Add testimonials/review count
- **Urgency**: Add ethical scarcity (limited spots, timer)
- **Page Speed**: Compress images, enable caching
- **Checkout**: Remove unnecessary form fields
- **Email Subject**: Test curiosity vs benefit-driven

**Characteristics**: Low effort, high impact, reversible, testable in days

### Strategic Fixes (Plan for 2-4 Weeks)
- **Funnel Redesign**: Rebuild conversion path
- **Product Positioning**: Revise messaging and offer
- **Platform Diversification**: Launch new traffic channel
- **Content Strategy**: Complete format/topic pivot
- **Pricing Model**: Restructure pricing tiers
- **Customer Journey**: Implement retention system

**Characteristics**: High effort, high impact, requires planning, testable in weeks

## Data Requirements

### Minimum Data Needed
- **Traffic**: 30 days of analytics data
- **Conversions**: At least 20 transactions
- **Sources**: Traffic source breakdown
- **Behavior**: Bounce rate, time on page

### Ideal Data Set
- **Traffic**: 90+ days for trend analysis
- **Conversions**: 100+ for statistical testing
- **Customer**: Survey responses, support tickets
- **Competitors**: Benchmark data from similar businesses
- **Cohorts**: Customer behavior by acquisition date

### When Data Is Insufficient
- **Use qualitative research**: Customer interviews, surveys
- **Benchmark against industry**: Research published niche averages
- **Start tracking now**: Implement analytics before optimizing
- **Focus on obvious issues**: Fix clear problems while collecting data

## Diagnosis Process

1. **Gather Metrics**: Collect last 30-90 days of data
2. **Calculate Rates**: Compute conversion rates for each funnel stage
3. **Compare Benchmarks**: Identify stages below industry average
4. **Rank by Impact**: Calculate potential gain if fixed
5. **Assess Effort**: Estimate implementation difficulty
6. **Prioritize**: Focus on highest impact-to-effort ratio
7. **Select Play**: Choose optimization from playbook
8. **Test & Measure**: Implement, track, analyze

## Example Diagnosis

**Scenario**: Digital product business

**Metrics**:
- Impressions: 50,000
- Engagement: 500 (1% rate) ✓ Benchmark met
- Clicks: 750 (1.5% CTR) ✗ Below 2% benchmark
- Landing visits: 750
- Purchases: 8 (1.07% conversion) ✗ Below 2% benchmark
- Repeat buyers: 1 (12.5% repeat rate) ✓ Above 10% benchmark

**Diagnosis**:
- **Primary Bottleneck**: Traffic (low CTR)
- **Secondary Bottleneck**: Conversion (low landing page rate)
- **Strength**: Retention (good repeat rate)

**Impact Calculation**:
- Fix CTR to 2.5%: +67% more visitors = +5 sales
- Fix conversion to 2%: +87% more sales from same traffic = +7 sales
- **Winner**: Fix conversion (higher impact)

**Recommendation**:
1. **High Priority**: Landing page conversion optimization
2. **Medium Priority**: CTR improvement testing
3. **Monitor**: Continue tracking retention

**Action Plan**:
- Week 1: Add social proof to landing page
- Week 2: A/B test headline variations
- Week 3: Simplify checkout process
- Week 4: Test new CTA copy for traffic
