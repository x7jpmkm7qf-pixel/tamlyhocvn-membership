# Offer Stack Builder Template

## What is an Offer Stack?

An offer stack transforms a simple product into an irresistible package by combining multiple valuable components and presenting their total worth against a discounted actual price.

**Core Purpose**: Increase perceived value far beyond actual price, making the purchase decision obvious.

**Formula**: Core Offer + Strategic Bonuses + Risk Reversal + Urgency = Irresistible Stack

---

## Offer Stack Anatomy

### 1. Core Offer (The Main Thing)
**Purpose**: The primary solution to the customer's main problem

**Questions to Answer**:
- What specific outcome does this deliver?
- What format is it delivered in?
- How long does it take to consume/implement?
- What makes it unique or superior?

**Example Core Offers**:
- Complete online course with 40 video lessons
- 90-day coaching program with weekly calls
- Software tool with full feature access
- Done-for-you service package
- Certification program with exam

**Pricing Consideration**: This should be valued at 60-70% of total stack value

---

### 2. Bonus 1 - Time Saver
**Purpose**: Accelerate implementation, reduce effort required

**Types**:
- **Templates**: Fill-in-the-blank resources
- **Checklists**: Step-by-step workflows
- **Scripts**: Copy-paste solutions
- **Tools**: Software or calculators
- **Swipe files**: Proven examples to model

**Value Positioning**: "This alone would take you 20+ hours to create"

**Examples**:
- 50 email templates ($297 value)
- Social media content calendar with 90 days of posts ($497 value)
- Contract templates library ($197 value)
- Automation workflow blueprints ($397 value)

**Pricing Consideration**: Value at 10-15% of total stack

---

### 3. Bonus 2 - Skill Builder
**Purpose**: Build competence, increase likelihood of success

**Types**:
- **Training modules**: Complementary skills
- **Masterclasses**: Deep dives on specific topics
- **Case studies**: Real-world examples
- **Interviews**: Expert insights
- **Workshops**: Interactive sessions

**Value Positioning**: "Master the foundational skills that guarantee results"

**Examples**:
- Copywriting fundamentals mini-course ($197 value)
- Sales call training ($397 value)
- Traffic generation workshop ($297 value)
- Mindset mastery program ($247 value)

**Pricing Consideration**: Value at 10-15% of total stack

---

### 4. Bonus 3 - Support & Community
**Purpose**: Reduce perceived risk, provide accountability

**Types**:
- **Private community**: Peer support and networking
- **Q&A sessions**: Live or recorded
- **Office hours**: Direct access to experts
- **Implementation support**: Technical help
- **Accountability groups**: Progress tracking

**Value Positioning**: "Never feel stuck or alone on your journey"

**Examples**:
- 90-day private Telegram/Discord access ($497 value)
- Monthly group coaching calls ($997 value)
- Email support for 6 months ($397 value)
- Weekly accountability check-ins ($697 value)

**Pricing Consideration**: Value at 15-20% of total stack

---

### 5. Bonus 4 - Fast-Action Bonus
**Purpose**: Create urgency, reward immediate decision-makers

**Requirements**:
- **Time-limited**: Only for orders within X days/hours
- **Exclusive**: Not available anywhere else
- **High perceived value**: Worth the rush
- **Easy to deliver**: Digital/automated

**Types**:
- One-on-one consultation call
- Priority access or early entry
- Additional training module
- VIP upgrade or special status
- Limited edition resource

**Value Positioning**: "Only for the first 50 buyers" or "Expires in 48 hours"

**Examples**:
- 30-minute strategy call with expert ($497 value)
- VIP fast-track implementation guide ($297 value)
- Exclusive industry report ($197 value)
- Lifetime updates vs annual renewal ($397 value)

**Pricing Consideration**: Value at 10-15% of total stack

---

### 6. Guarantee - Risk Reversal
**Purpose**: Remove fear of making wrong decision, increase conversions

**Common Types**:

**A. Money-Back Guarantee**
- "30-day 100% money-back guarantee"
- "60-day no-questions-asked refund"
- "Try it risk-free for 90 days"

**B. Results Guarantee**
- "Get results in 30 days or full refund"
- "Double your money back if you don't see X result"
- "Work with us until you succeed"

**C. Better-Than-Money-Back**
- "Keep everything even if you refund"
- "Get full refund + extra $100 for your time"
- "Free upgrade to next tier if unsatisfied"

**Vietnamese Market Considerations**:
- Trust is harder to build, stronger guarantees needed
- Consider "satisfaction guaranteed" messaging
- Payment installment plans reduce perceived risk
- Social proof (testimonials) as trust enhancer

**How to Present**:
```
"I'm so confident this will work for you that I'm taking on ALL the risk.
Try [Product] for 60 days. If you don't see [specific result], I'll refund
every penny—no questions asked. You literally cannot lose."
```

## The Value Equation

**Framework**: Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort Required)

### How to Maximize Value

**Increase Dream Outcome**:
- Paint vivid picture of transformation
- Use specific numbers and results
- Show before/after examples
- Highlight emotional benefits

**Increase Perceived Likelihood**:
- Provide case studies and testimonials
- Show your credentials/proof
- Demonstrate the system/process
- Offer guarantee to remove risk

**Decrease Time Delay**:
- Promise quick wins
- Show fast-track methods
- Provide done-for-you elements
- Offer templates and shortcuts

**Decrease Effort Required**:
- Simplify the process
- Provide step-by-step guidance
- Include support and community
- Give tools and automation

## Calculating Perceived Value

### Research-Based Valuation

**Method 1: Market Comparison**
- Find 3-5 similar products in market
- Average their prices
- Use that as your component value

**Method 2: Time Savings**
- Estimate hours saved by using your solution
- Multiply by hourly rate of target customer
- Example: 20 hours saved × $100/hr = $2,000 value

**Method 3: Replacement Cost**
- What would it cost to get this elsewhere?
- Use actual prices from alternatives
- Example: Hiring consultant for $150/hr × 10 hrs = $1,500

**Method 4: Opportunity Value**
- What revenue/profit will this generate?
- Use conservative estimate
- Example: Strategy generating $10K = valued at $2,500

### Credibility Rules
- Don't inflate values beyond believability
- Be able to justify each valuation
- Use real market prices as anchors
- Consider your audience's budget context

## Pro Tips

1. **Lead with core offer outcome**, not features
2. **Name bonuses creatively** (not "Bonus 1")—use benefit-driven names
3. **Vary bonus types** (digital, live, community, tools)
4. **Make bonuses complementary**, not redundant
5. **Test different stack combinations** with audience
6. **Update fast-action bonuses** regularly to maintain urgency
7. **Use testimonials** for each stack component when possible
8. **Consider payment plans** to lower entry barrier
9. **Stack should solve complete journey**, not just one step
10. **Deliver on promises**—reputation depends on satisfaction
