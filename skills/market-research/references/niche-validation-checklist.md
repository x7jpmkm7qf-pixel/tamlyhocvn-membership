# Niche Validation Checklist

## Overview
10-point validation scorecard to determine if your niche is profitable and viable in the Vietnamese market before investing significant time and money.

**Scoring System:** Each criterion scored 0-10 points
- **80-100 points:** Strong Go — High potential niche
- **60-79 points:** Cautious Go — Viable with strategic positioning
- **40-59 points:** Risky — Requires significant pivoting
- **Below 40:** No Go — Choose different niche

---

## Validation Checklist

### 1. Market Demand Signals (0-10 points)

**What to Check:**
- Google Trends Vietnam (trending up or stable?)
- Facebook group activity (active discussions daily?)
- YouTube search volume (Vietnamese content available?)
- TikTok hashtag views (millions+ views?)

**Scoring Guide:**
- **10 points:** 10K+ monthly searches, multiple active FB groups (1K+ members), trending hashtags
- **5 points:** 1-5K monthly searches, few active groups, some content
- **0 points:** <500 searches, no active communities, dead topic

**Quick Tests:**
```
✓ Search "[your niche] việt nam" on Google
✓ Search Facebook groups: "[niche] vietnam"
✓ Check TikTok hashtag: #[niche]vn
✓ YouTube: "[niche] hướng dẫn tiếng việt"
```

### 2. Audience Willingness to Pay (0-10 points)

**What to Check:**

- Payment evidence (reviews, testimonials, sales posts)
- Problem severity (painful vs nice-to-have)

- **10 points:** Multiple paid products 500K+, visible buyer testimonials, urgent problem
- **5 points:** Few paid products, mostly free content, moderate problem
- **0 points:** No paid products, only free content, low-priority problem


### 3. Competition Level Analysis (0-10 points)

**What to Check:**
- Number of established competitors (saturation level)
- Quality of existing content (professional or amateur?)
- Competitor engagement rates (high or low?)
- Barriers to entry (technical skills required?)

**Scoring Guide:**
- **10 points:** 3-10 competitors, moderate quality, opportunities for differentiation
- **5 points:** 20+ competitors OR <3 competitors (too saturated or too risky)
- **0 points:** 50+ competitors with high quality OR 0 competitors (no market)

**Sweet Spot:** Goldilocks zone — not too crowded, not too empty

**Quick Tests:**
```
✓ Facebook Page search: How many pages in your niche?
✓ Instagram: Top 5 accounts — what's their follower count?
✓ YouTube: Top 3 channels — how many subscribers?
✓ Assess content quality: Could you do better?
```

## Decision Framework

### 80-100 Points: STRONG GO ✅
**Action Plan:**
1. Proceed with confidence to content creation
2. Invest in paid tools/ads (budget 3-5M VND)
3. Build flagship product within 30 days
4. Launch with paid traffic campaign

**Expected Timeline:** 60-90 days to first sales

---

### 60-79 Points: CAUTIOUS GO ⚠️
**Action Plan:**
1. Proceed but validate MVP first (minimum viable product)
2. Start with free content/organic growth
3. Test small paid product (297-497K) before flagship
4. Budget 1-2M VND for initial testing

**Expected Timeline:** 90-120 days to consistent income

**Red Flags to Watch:**
- If scores below 5 on: Willingness to Pay OR Monetization Potential → Rethink pricing/positioning
- If score below 5 on: Your Expertise Match → Get results/experience first

---

### 40-59 Points: RISKY — PIVOT REQUIRED ⛔
**Action Plan:**
1. DO NOT create products yet
2. Identify your 3 lowest scores
3. Pivot niche angle/audience to improve those scores
4. Re-validate after pivot

**Pivot Strategies:**
```
Low Market Demand → Niche down to sub-segment with higher demand
Low Willingness to Pay → Target different audience segment (business vs consumer)
High Competition → Find unique positioning angle (faster, cheaper, specialized)
Low Expertise → Partner with expert OR spend 3 months gaining experience
```

---

### Below 40 Points: NO GO 🛑
**Action Plan:**
1. Choose a completely different niche
2. Use this checklist to validate new niche
3. DO NOT proceed with current idea

**Why This Matters:**
Pursuing a low-scoring niche = wasted months + money with minimal results. Better to pivot early than fail late.

## Vietnamese Market Red Flags

**Immediate Disqualifiers:**
- ❌ No existing Vietnamese content/communities (too early or no market)
- ❌ Only expensive international products (price mismatch for VN buyers)
- ❌ Requires illegal/grey-area activities (regulatory risk)
- ❌ Extremely technical with no Vietnamese resources (high barrier)
- ❌ Target audience not on Facebook/TikTok/YouTube (unreachable)

**Proceed with Extreme Caution:**
- ⚠️ Dominated by 1-2 major players with large followings (hard to compete)
- ⚠️ Trending topic with no proven monetization (hype vs sustainable)
- ⚠️ International trend not adapted to VN context (cultural mismatch)
- ⚠️ Audience willing to pay but only <100K (low margins)

### Example 1: "Facebook Ads for Small Business" (Score: 85/100)
- Market Demand: 9/10 (high search volume)
- Willingness to Pay: 9/10 (businesses paying 2-5M for courses)
- Competition: 7/10 (10-15 active creators, room for specialization)

- Expertise: 8/10 (2 years experience running campaigns)
- Urgency: 10/10 (businesses need customers NOW)

**Decision:** STRONG GO — High-demand B2B niche with multiple revenue streams

### Example 2: "AI Art Generation Tutorials" (Score: 52/100)
