# Email Welcome Sequence (7 Emails)

## Overview
Automated email sequence for new subscribers who download your lead magnet. Goal: build trust, deliver value, introduce paid offer.

**Sequence Timeline:** 12 days (Email 1 = immediate, then spaced over 12 days)

**Open Rate Targets:**
- Email 1: 60-80% (delivery email)
- Emails 2-4: 40-60% (value-building)
- Emails 5-7: 30-50% (sales emails)

**Overall Conversion Goal:** 5-10% of subscribers take action on paid offer by Email 7

---

## Email 1 — Immediate: Delivery + Quick Win

**Send Time:** Immediately after opt-in (automated trigger)

**Subject Line Options:**
- "Here's your [Lead Magnet Name] + a bonus tip"
- "[Lead Magnet] is ready — download now"
- "Welcome! Your [Lead Magnet] is inside"

**Email Body Structure:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Welcome! Thanks for grabbing [Lead Magnet Name].

→ [DOWNLOAD LINK - BIG BUTTON]

Quick tip to get started right now:
[One actionable tip from the lead magnet — give value immediately]

Example: "Open the guide and start with page 3. That's the fastest way to see results today."

What to expect from me:
- [Frequency]: I'll email you [X] times per week
- [Content]: Tips, strategies, and stories to help you [outcome]
- [Promise]: No spam, no BS. Unsubscribe anytime.

Question: What's your biggest challenge with [topic]? Hit reply and let me know. I read every email.

Talk soon,
[Your Name]

P.S. Can't find the email with your download? Check your spam folder and mark this as "Not Spam."
```

**Key Elements:**
- Clear download link (button, not just text)
- Quick win tip (show value immediately)
- Set expectations (frequency, content type)
- Ask a question (encourage reply for engagement)
- P.S. reminder (reduce support requests)

## Email 2 — Day 1: Story + Credibility

**Send Time:** 24 hours after Email 1

**Subject Line Options:**
- "Why I created [Lead Magnet Name]"
- "The story behind [Lead Magnet]"
- "How I went from [struggle] to [success]"

**Email Body Structure:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Quick question: Did you download [Lead Magnet] yet?

If not, [reinsert download link]. If yes, awesome. Let me tell you why I made it.

[Story — 3 paragraphs]:

Paragraph 1: The Problem I Had
"[Time period] ago, I was struggling with [exact problem your audience faces]. I tried [common solutions], but nothing worked. I felt [emotion]."

Paragraph 2: The Discovery
"Then I discovered [solution/method]. It wasn't easy at first, but I committed to [specific action]. Within [timeframe], I saw [specific result]."

Paragraph 3: The Mission
"I created [Lead Magnet] because I wish I had this when I started. It would have saved me [time/money/pain]. Now I want to help you skip the mistakes I made."

That's my story. What's yours?

Reply and tell me: Where are you right now with [topic]? Stuck? Making progress? Let me know.

[Your Name]

P.S. Tomorrow I'm sending you [teaser for Email 3]. Stay tuned.
```

**Key Elements:**
- Personal story (builds relatability)
- Vulnerability (shows you're human)
- Authority (subtle proof of results)
- Future pacing (hint at next email)

## Email 3 — Day 3: Value Content

**Send Time:** 3 days after Email 1 (48 hours after Email 2)

**Subject Line Options:**
- "[Actionable tip related to lead magnet topic]"
- "The #1 way to [achieve specific outcome]"
- "Try this: [specific tactic]"

**Email Body Structure:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Here's something you can use today:

[Main Teaching — 4-5 short paragraphs or bullet list]

The Problem:
[Describe a specific sub-problem within your niche]

The Solution:
[Teach one actionable tactic — step-by-step]

Example:
"Here's how I did this:
- Step 1: [Action]
- Step 2: [Action]
- Step 3: [Action]
Result: [Outcome in specific timeframe]"

Your Turn:
[Give them a small challenge or action step]
"Try this today and reply to tell me how it went. I'll personally respond."

See you in your inbox soon,
[Your Name]

P.S. If you found this helpful, share it with someone who needs it. Forward this email or tag them.
```

**Key Elements:**
- Pure value (no pitch)
- Tactical and specific (not generic advice)
- Encourage engagement (reply, share)
- Short and actionable (under 300 words)

**Send Time:** 5 days after Email 1

**Subject Line Options:**
- "How [Customer Type] achieved [Result]"
- "This [job/role] went from [before] to [after]"
- "[Name]'s story will inspire you"

**Email Body Structure:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Let me introduce you to [Customer Name or "a student/client"].

[Case Study — Story Format]:

Where They Started:
"[Name] was [struggling with X]. They had tried [failed attempts]. Sound familiar?"

What They Did:
"They used [your method/product] and focused on [specific actions]. No shortcuts, just consistent effort."

The Results:
"In [timeframe], they achieved [specific outcome]:
- [Metric 1]: Increased by X%
- [Metric 2]: Went from Y to Z
- [Metric 3]: Saved/earned $[amount]"

The Lesson:
"[Name]'s secret? They [key insight]. You can do the same."

[Optional: Link to full case study or testimonial]

What's possible for you?

Think about where you want to be in [timeframe]. Then take one small step today.

[Your Name]

P.S. Want results like this? Stay tuned. I've got something coming that can help.
```

**Key Elements:**
- Specific results (numbers, timeframes)
- Relatable starting point (like your audience)
- Subtle foreshadowing of offer
- Inspire action (not just passive reading)

## Email 5 — Day 7: Problem Awareness

**Send Time:** 7 days after Email 1 (one week mark)

**Subject Line Options:**
- "The #1 mistake [audience] makes with [topic]"
- "Why [common approach] doesn't work"
- "Stop doing this (it's costing you [outcome])"

**Email Body Structure:**

```
Hi [First Name],

I see this mistake everywhere:

[The Mistake]:
"[Audience] thinks they need to [common wrong approach]. So they spend [time/money] on [ineffective solution]."

Why It Fails:
"Here's the problem: [Explain why common approach doesn't work]. The real issue is [root cause]."

The Consequences:
"If you keep doing this, you'll:
- [Negative outcome 1]
- [Negative outcome 2]
- [Negative outcome 3]

I know because I made this exact mistake. It cost me [specific pain]."

The Better Way:
"Instead, focus on [your solution approach]. This works because [reason]."

[Bridge to Offer]:
"I've helped [number] people avoid this mistake using [method/product]. If you're serious about [outcome], I have something that might help."

More details coming in your next email.

[Your Name]

P.S. Quick question: Are you still making this mistake? Reply and tell me honestly.
```

**Key Elements:**
- Problem agitation (make pain clear)
- Position common solution as insufficient
- Bridge to your solution (without pitching yet)
- Tease upcoming offer

[Optional: Bonuses, guarantee, limited spots]

✅ You if [criteria 1]
✅ You if [criteria 2]
❌ NOT for you if [criteria — disqualify bad fits]

[Call to Action]:
"Ready to start? Here's the link: [SALES PAGE LINK]

- Recap value delivered (justify ask)

- Qualification criteria (self-select)
- Low-pressure tone (soft pitch)
- Link to sales page (not checkout yet)

**Subject Line Options:**

**Email Body Structure:**

```

[If quantity-limited]: Only [number] spots left.

Here's what you're getting:
→ [Benefit 1]
→ [Benefit 2]
→ [Benefit 3]
→ [Bonus items if any]

The Investment: [Price]
The Value: [Higher value or ROI potential]

[Guarantee]:
"Try it for [timeframe]. If you don't see [result], I'll refund 100%. No questions."

[Final CTA]:
"If you're ready to [outcome], click here now: [CHECKOUT LINK]"

Two paths from here:

Path 1: You join [Product Name] and start [outcome] within [timeframe].
Path 2: You pass on this, and we stay connected through my free emails.

Either way, no hard feelings.

I'm here to help however I can.

[Your Name]

P.S. Questions? Reply before [deadline]. I'll get back to you ASAP.
```

**Key Elements:**
- Urgency (deadline, scarcity)
- Recap value (benefits, guarantee)
- Direct link to checkout (reduce friction)
- Acknowledge choice (respect autonomy)
- Open door to questions (reduce objections)

## Subject Line Optimization Tips

**High Open Rate Formulas:**
- Curiosity: "The thing I forgot to mention..."
- Personalization: "[First Name], quick question for you"
- Numbers: "3 mistakes costing you [outcome]"
- Urgency: "Only [time/spots] left"
- Social Proof: "How [Name] achieved [Result]"

**Avoid Spam Triggers:**
- ALL CAPS
- Excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Words like FREE, GUARANTEE, CLICK HERE in subject
- Misleading claims

**A/B Testing:**
- Test 2 subject lines per email with 10% of list
- Winner goes to remaining 90%
- Track: open rate, click rate, reply rate

---

## Engagement Tips

**Increase Reply Rates:**
- Ask specific questions (not generic "how are you?")
- Make it easy to reply (one-word answers OK)
- Reply to every response (builds relationship)

**Increase Click Rates:**
- Use buttons, not just text links
- Repeat CTA 2-3 times in email (top, middle, bottom)
- Make benefit of clicking clear

**Reduce Unsubscribes:**
- Set expectations in Email 1 (frequency, content)
- Segment based on engagement (separate promotions from content)
- Give option to reduce frequency (vs full unsubscribe)
