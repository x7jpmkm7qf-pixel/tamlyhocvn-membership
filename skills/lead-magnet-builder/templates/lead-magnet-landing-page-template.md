# Lead Magnet Landing Page Template

## Section 1: Hero (Above the Fold)

**Headline Formula:**
```
[NUMBER] [ADJECTIVE] Ways to [DESIRED OUTCOME] Without [PAIN POINT]
```

**Examples:**
- "5 Proven Ways to Get Your First 100 Customers Without Paid Ads"
- "7 Simple Strategies to Build a Profitable Side Business Without Quitting Your Job"
- "3 Powerful Templates to Write Converting Sales Copy Without Hiring a Copywriter"

**Subheadline:**
```
Download this free [LEAD_MAGNET_TYPE] and start [BENEFIT] today
```

**Examples:**
- "Download this free guide and start generating leads in the next 7 days"
- "Download this free checklist and start saving 10 hours per week today"
- "Download this free template bundle and start creating professional content immediately"

**CTA Button:**
```
"Get My Free [LEAD_MAGNET_TYPE]"
```
- Color: High contrast (orange, green, red against white/light background)
- Size: Large, thumb-friendly (mobile optimization)
- Position: Centered, above fold

**Visual:**
- Mockup image of lead magnet (ebook cover, checklist screenshot, template preview)
- Use Canva to create professional mockup
- Show 3D book/document visual for perceived value
- Alt text for accessibility

**Full Hero Section HTML Structure:**
```html
<section id="hero">
  <h1>[Headline]</h1>
  <h2>[Subheadline]</h2>
  <img src="[mockup.png]" alt="[Lead Magnet Name] preview">
  <button>[CTA Button Text]</button>
</section>
```

## Section 2: Problem Statement

**Purpose:** Agitate the pain point, create urgency for solution

**Structure:**
```
"Are you struggling with [PROBLEM]? You're not alone.

[STATISTIC] of [AUDIENCE] face this exact challenge every day.

The problem? [ROOT CAUSE DESCRIPTION]

Without [solution], you'll keep [negative consequence]."
```

**Example:**
```
"Are you struggling to grow your email list? You're not alone.

73% of small business owners say email marketing is their biggest challenge.

The problem? You're creating content, but you have nothing compelling to offer in exchange for an email address.

Without a high-value lead magnet, you'll keep posting on social media with nothing to show for it—no list, no sales, no growth."
```

**Best Practices:**
- Use real statistics (cite source if possible)
- Mirror audience language (use their words)
- Build emotional connection (frustration, fear, hope)
- Keep under 100 words

---

## Section 3: What You'll Get

**Purpose:** List clear benefits (not features) of the lead magnet

**Structure:**
```
Inside [Lead Magnet Name], you'll discover:

✅ [BENEFIT_1]: [One-line description of outcome]
✅ [BENEFIT_2]: [One-line description of outcome]
✅ [BENEFIT_3]: [One-line description of outcome]
✅ BONUS: [BONUS_ITEM with unexpected extra value]
```

**Example:**
```
Inside the "List Building Toolkit", you'll discover:

✅ 5 Lead Magnet Ideas That Convert at 40%+: Proven formats for your niche
✅ Plug-and-Play Landing Page Template: Copy-paste and customize in 10 minutes
✅ Email Sequence Scripts: 7 pre-written emails to nurture subscribers
✅ BONUS: 50 Magnetic Subject Lines to boost open rates by 30%
```

**Writing Tips:**
- Lead with the outcome, not the feature
  - Bad: "A 20-page PDF guide"
  - Good: "The exact 5-step system to [outcome]"
- Use numbers (specific > vague)
- Include a bonus (increases perceived value)
- Keep each bullet to one line (scannable)

## Section 4: Social Proof

**Purpose:** Build credibility and trust

**Elements:**

**Download Count:**
```
"[NUMBER]+ people have already downloaded this [LEAD_MAGNET_TYPE]"
```
- Use real number (even if "50+" is better than nothing)
- Update regularly as downloads grow

**Testimonial:**
```
"[QUOTE]"
— [FIRST NAME + ROLE], [COMPANY_TYPE or LOCATION]
```

**Example Testimonial:**
```
"This checklist saved me 5 hours in my first week. I wish I found this sooner!"
— Sarah, Freelance Designer
```

**Alternative Social Proof (if no testimonials yet):**
- "As featured in [Publication/Podcast]"
- "Used by [Type of People] in [Number] countries"
- "Created by [Your Credential/Authority]"

**Visual:**
- Small headshot or logo (builds trust)
- Star rating if applicable
- Screenshot of result (before/after, metrics)

## Section 5: About / Credibility

**Purpose:** Answer "Why should I trust you?"

**Structure:**
```
Hi, I'm [YOUR_NAME/BRAND].

I'm a [CREDENTIAL/ROLE] who has helped [NUMBER]+ [AUDIENCE] achieve [RESULT].

[Short story — 2-3 sentences]:
[Timeframe] ago, I was [relatable struggle]. After [journey], I discovered [solution]. Now I help [audience] do the same.

This [lead magnet] is the exact [resource type] I wish I had when I started.
```

**Example:**
```
Hi, I'm Mai Nguyen.

I'm a content strategist who has helped 200+ solopreneurs build profitable online businesses without paid ads.

Three years ago, I was stuck at a 9-5 job with a side hustle going nowhere. After cracking the code on organic content and email marketing, I scaled to full-time income in 8 months. Now I help Vietnamese entrepreneurs do the same.

This toolkit is the exact system I wish I had when I started.
```

**Best Practices:**
- Use a photo (real face builds trust 35% more)
- Keep it humble but authoritative
- Make it relatable (they see themselves in your story)
- Under 75 words

## Section 6: FAQ (3-4 Questions)

**Purpose:** Remove objections, clarify expectations

**Question 1: Is this really free?**
```
Q: Is this really free?
A: Yes! 100% free. No credit card required. No hidden costs. Just enter your email and download immediately.
```

**Question 2: Who is this for?**
```
Q: Who is this for?
A: This is perfect for [specific audience description]. Whether you're [scenario 1] or [scenario 2], this will help you [outcome].
```

**Example:**
```
Q: Who is this for?
A: This is perfect for coaches, consultants, and freelancers. Whether you're just starting or have an established business, this will help you grow your email list fast.
```

**Question 3: How do I access it?**
```
Q: How will I receive the [lead magnet]?
A: Instantly! After entering your email, you'll get immediate access to download the [PDF/template/etc.]. It will also be sent to your inbox (check spam if you don't see it).
```

**Question 4 (Optional): What will you do with my email?**
```
Q: What will you do with my email?
A: I'll send you helpful tips about [topic] via email [frequency]. No spam. Unsubscribe anytime with one click. I respect your inbox.
```

**Best Practices:**
- Address most common objections (free, relevance, delivery)
- Keep answers short (1-2 sentences)
- Use conversational tone

## Section 7: Final CTA

**Purpose:** Repeat the offer, make it impossible to miss

**Structure:**
```
[Repeat Headline or Variation]

[CTA Button - same as hero]

[Trust Statement]
```

**Example:**
```
Ready to Grow Your Email List?

[Get My Free Toolkit Now]

100% free. No credit card required. Instant access.
```

**Best Practices:**
- Large button (same color as hero CTA)
- Add urgency if applicable: "Download Now Before This Page Closes"
- Include micro-trust signals:
  - "🔒 Your email is safe. Unsubscribe anytime."
  - "⚡ Instant delivery to your inbox"
  - "✅ Join [number]+ subscribers"

## Full Page Layout Flow (Mobile-First)

```
1. Hero (Headline + Subheadline + Visual + CTA)
   ↓
2. Problem Statement (Agitate pain)
   ↓
3. What You'll Get (Benefits list)
   ↓
4. Social Proof (Testimonial + Download count)
   ↓
5. About (Quick credibility)
   ↓
6. FAQ (Remove objections)
   ↓
7. Final CTA (Repeat offer + button)
```

**Page Length:** 1-2 scrolls on mobile (500-800 words total)

## Design Best Practices

### Mobile Responsive (60%+ of traffic)
- Single column layout
- Large tap targets (buttons 44x44px minimum)
- Readable font size (16px+ body text)
- Fast loading (compress images, under 1MB total page weight)

### Color Psychology
- **Blue:** Trust, professionalism (financial, B2B)
- **Green:** Growth, health (wellness, eco)
- **Orange/Red:** Urgency, action (limited offers, events)
- **Purple:** Creativity, luxury (design, coaching)

### Typography
- Headline: Bold, 32-48px (mobile), 48-72px (desktop)
- Subheadline: 18-24px
- Body: 16-18px, line height 1.5-1.7
- Font: Sans-serif for readability (Open Sans, Roboto, Inter)

### White Space
- Generous padding around sections (40-60px)
- Breathing room around CTA button
- Don't cram content

## Email Capture Form

**Minimalist Form (Higher Conversion):**
```
[Email input field]
[Submit Button: "Get My Free [Lead Magnet]"]

Privacy note: "We respect your privacy. Unsubscribe anytime."
```

**Only ask for:**
- Email (required)
- First name (optional, for personalization)

**Do NOT ask for:**
- Phone number (reduces conversion 20-30%)
- Last name (unnecessary)
- Company (unless B2B qualification needed)

**Form Placement:**
- Hero section (immediate visibility)
- After FAQ (after objections removed)
- Sticky footer (mobile) or sidebar (desktop)

## Testing & Optimization

**A/B Test:**
- Headline variations (test 2-3)
- CTA button color (orange vs green vs red)
- Testimonial (with/without photo)
- Form length (email only vs email+name)

**Metrics to Track:**
- Page views
- Conversion rate (opt-ins / page views)
- Bounce rate (under 50% is good)
- Time on page (1-2 min average)

**Target Benchmarks:**
- Conversion rate: 20-40% (cold traffic), 40-60% (warm traffic)
- Mobile load time: Under 3 seconds
- Email deliverability: Above 95%

**Tools:**
- Google Analytics (traffic tracking)
- Hotjar (heatmaps, scroll depth)
- Email service provider (conversion tracking)

## Template Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

✅ Headline clearly states outcome + removes pain point
✅ CTA button visible above fold (no scrolling needed)
✅ Benefits listed (not just features)
✅ Social proof included (testimonial or download count)
✅ Mobile-responsive (test on phone)
✅ Email form connected to email service provider
✅ Thank you page/email set up for delivery
✅ Privacy policy linked (if required by region)
✅ Page loads fast (under 3 seconds)
✅ No broken links or images
