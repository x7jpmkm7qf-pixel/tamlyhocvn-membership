# Social Post Templates Library

## Facebook Templates

### Template 1: Story Post

**Structure**: Personal story + lesson learned + question

**Character Limit**: 40-80 words (optimal for readability)

**Hook Examples**:
- "Three years ago, I made a mistake that cost me $10,000..."
- "Nobody tells you this about starting a business..."
- "I used to believe [common myth], until..."

**Template**:
```
[Hook: Attention-grabbing opening]

[Story: 2-3 sentences of narrative]

[Lesson: What you learned or realized]

[Question: Engagement prompt]
```

**Example**:
```
I used to spend 4 hours planning my week, then wonder why I never got anything done.

Turned out I was confusing planning with actually working. The real breakthrough came when I cut planning to 30 minutes every Sunday and just started executing.

Now I get more done by Wednesday than I used to in full weeks.

What's one thing you plan too much instead of just doing?
```

**CTA Options**:
- "Drop your answer below"
- "Tell me in the comments"
- "Share your experience"
- "Link to [resource] in comments"

### Template 2: Value Post

**Structure**: Problem + solution framework + actionable steps

**Character Limit**: 60-100 words

**Hook Examples**:
- "Stop doing [common action]. Here's what works instead..."
- "You don't need [expensive solution]. Try this..."
- "The fastest way to [desired outcome] nobody talks about..."

**Template**:
```
[Hook: Problem statement or contrarian take]

[Framework: Name the solution or method]

Here's how:
→ [Step 1]
→ [Step 2]
→ [Step 3]

[Result statement]
```

**Example**:
```
Stop creating content every single day. Start batching.

The Content Batch System:
→ Pick one day per week
→ Create 7 posts in one sitting
→ Schedule everything in advance
→ Spend other days engaging and growing

You'll save 5+ hours weekly and your content will actually improve because you're in creation mode, not scrambling mode.

Try it next week.
```

**CTA Options**:
- "Save this for later"
- "Try it and report back"
- "Want the full framework? Comment [keyword]"

### Template 3: Engagement Post

**Structure**: This or that question + your take + invite discussion

**Character Limit**: 30-50 words (keep it short for maximum engagement)

**Hook Examples**:
- "Controversial opinion:"
- "Hot take:"
- "Unpopular but true:"

**Template**:
```
[Hook: Attention grab]

[Option A] vs [Option B]?

I'm team [your choice] because [brief reason].

What about you?
```

**Example**:
```
Controversial opinion:

Consistency beats strategy every time.

I'd rather see someone post mediocre content daily than brilliant content once a month. Momentum matters more than perfection when you're building an audience.

Agree or disagree?
```

**CTA Options**:
- "Team A or Team B?"
- "Change my mind"
- "Defend your position below"

## TikTok Templates

### Template 1: Tutorial Hook

**Structure**: Problem statement + step-by-step + result promise

**Video Length**: 30-60 seconds

**Hook Examples**:
- "If you're struggling with [problem], do this..."
- "Stop [wrong method]. Try this instead..."
- "How to [outcome] in under 60 seconds..."

**Template**:
```
Visual: [On-screen you or screen recording]
Audio: Hook + quick intro

Step 1: [Show action]
Step 2: [Show action]
Step 3: [Show action]

Result: [Show outcome]
CTA: [Follow/save/comment prompt]
```

**Script Example**:
```
Hook: "Your captions are killing your engagement. Here's why."

Step 1: [Show bad caption] "Most people write like this — boring, no hook, no reason to care."

Step 2: [Show improved version] "Instead, start with a question or bold statement."

Step 3: [Show engagement proof] "Same post, new caption, 4x more comments."

CTA: "Follow for more content tips."
```

**Overlay Text**:
- Line 1: Hook statement
- Lines 2-4: Steps (one per screen)
- Line 5: Result or CTA
