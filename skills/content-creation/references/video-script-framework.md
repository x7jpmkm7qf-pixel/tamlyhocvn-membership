# Video Script Frameworks for Short-Form Content

## Platform Length Guidelines

### TikTok
**Optimal Length:** 15-60 seconds
- 15-30s: Trending sounds, quick tips, memes
- 30-60s: Tutorials, storytelling, product demos
- Sweet spot: 21-34 seconds (highest completion rate)

### Instagram Reels
**Optimal Length:** 30-90 seconds
- 30-45s: Quick value, hooks for attention
- 45-90s: Detailed tutorials, narratives
- Sweet spot: 45-60 seconds (balance depth + retention)

### YouTube Shorts
**Optimal Length:** 30-60 seconds
- Under 60s for Shorts algorithm optimization
- 45-59s: Maximum value before cutoff
- Sweet spot: 50-55 seconds

**General Rule:** Shorter = higher retention, but less depth. Test both.

## Framework 1: Hook → Story → Offer

**Structure Breakdown:**

**Hook (0-3 seconds):** Pattern interrupt
- Bold visual/movement
- Unexpected statement
- Direct question
- Curiosity gap

**Story (3-45 seconds):** Problem → Journey → Transformation
- Relatable problem (3-10s)
- Your attempt/struggle (10-25s)
- Discovery/solution (25-40s)
- Result/transformation (40-45s)

**Offer (Last 5-15 seconds):** CTA
- Clear next step (follow, comment, link in bio)
- Reason to act (value proposition)
- Urgency if applicable

**Example Script (60s):**
```
[0-3s] HOOK: "I wasted $5,000 before learning this simple trick..."
[3-15s] PROBLEM: "Last year I tried every marketing strategy—ads, SEO, influencers. Nothing worked."
[15-30s] JOURNEY: "Then a mentor told me: 'Stop selling. Start solving.' I created free guides..."
[30-45s] TRANSFORMATION: "Within 60 days, leads came to ME. No ads. Just value-first content."
[45-60s] OFFER: "Want my exact content playbook? Link in bio. It's free. Go grab it now."
```

## Framework 2: Problem → Amplify → Resolve

**Structure Breakdown:**

**Problem (0-10s):** State relatable pain point
- "You're struggling with [X]"
- "Ever feel like [frustration]?"
- Open loop—don't solve yet

**Amplify (10-35s):** Make pain vivid
- Consequences of ignoring problem
- Why common solutions fail
- Emotional impact

**Resolve (35-60s):** Reveal solution
- Your product/method as answer
- Quick proof (result, testimonial)
- Clear CTA

**Example Script (45s):**
```
[0-5s] PROBLEM: "Your Instagram posts get 20 likes and zero sales. I know the feeling."
[5-10s] AMPLIFY: "You post daily, use hashtags, still... crickets. Meanwhile, others blow up overnight."
[10-20s] MORE AMPLIFY: "Here's why: You're talking AT people, not WITH them. You're selling, not storytelling."
[20-35s] RESOLVE: "I switched to story-first posts. Same product, different frame. Result? 10x engagement."
[35-45s] CTA: "Drop a 🔥 if you want my 5 storytelling templates. They're in my bio for free."
```

## Framework 3: Tutorial/How-To

**Structure Breakdown:**

**Promise (0-5s):** "Here's how to [outcome] in [timeframe]"
- Specific outcome
- Time constraint creates urgency
- Visual text overlay of promise

**Step 1 (5-20s):** First actionable step
- Show, don't just tell
- Visual demonstration + voiceover

**Step 2 (20-35s):** Second step
- Build on Step 1
- Screen recording or product demo

**Step 3 (35-50s):** Final step
- Complete the process
- Show finished result

**CTA (50-60s):** Next action
- Follow for more tips
- Comment if helpful
- Link to full guide

**Example Script (60s Tutorial):**
```
[0-5s] PROMISE: "Here's how to get your first 100 email subscribers in 7 days."
[5-20s] STEP 1: "Day 1-2: Create a simple lead magnet. Mine is a 1-page checklist. Takes 30 minutes."
[20-35s] STEP 2: "Day 3-5: Share it on 3 platforms—Facebook group, LinkedIn post, IG story. Free traffic."
[35-50s] STEP 3: "Day 6-7: Email your subscribers once. Give value. Build trust. That's it."
[50-60s] CTA: "I broke down each day in my free guide. Link in bio. Go grab it and start today."
```

## Hook Formulas (First 3 Seconds)

**Pattern Interrupt Hooks:**
1. "Stop doing [common mistake]..."
2. "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]..."
3. "I made [impressive amount/result] by doing this..."
4. "[Number] things that changed my [business/life/results]..."
5. "POV: You just discovered [game-changing thing]..."

**Curiosity Hooks:**
6. "This [simple thing] made me [big result]. Here's how..."
7. "The secret to [outcome] isn't what you think..."
8. "I wish I knew this [timeframe] ago..."
9. "Everyone's doing [X] wrong. Do this instead..."
10. "If you're [struggling with Y], watch this..."

**Question Hooks:**
11. "Want to [achieve goal] without [pain point]?"
12. "Why do some [people] succeed while others fail?"
13. "Ever wonder how [impressive people] do [X]?"
14. "What if I told you [bold claim]?"
15. "Ready to [transformation] in [timeframe]?"

**Visual Hooks (No words needed first 1s):**
16. Sudden movement toward camera
17. Before/after side-by-side flash
18. Money/results on screen
19. Relatable frustrated face
20. Text overlay appearing with sound effect

## 5 Ready-to-Use Script Templates

### Template 1: The Transformation Story (60s)
```
[0-3s] HOOK: "From [bad state] to [good state] in [timeframe]. Here's my story."
[3-15s] BEFORE: "I was [struggling with X]. Every day felt like [pain description]."
[15-30s] TURNING POINT: "Then I tried [solution]. Skeptical at first, but [small win happened]."
[30-50s] AFTER: "Fast forward [timeframe]: [impressive results]. My [metric] went from [X] to [Y]."
[50-60s] CTA: "If you want [same result], I made a free guide. Link in bio. Let's do this together."
```

### Template 2: The Mistake Revealer (45s)
```
[0-3s] HOOK: "I wasted [time/money] on [common approach]. Don't make my mistake."
[3-15s] MISTAKE: "Everyone said to [bad advice]. So I did. Result? [negative outcome]."
[15-30s] REALIZATION: "Then I learned [contrarian truth]. Tried it. Everything changed."
[30-40s] PROOF: "[Specific result] in [timeframe]. All because I stopped [mistake]."
[40-45s] CTA: "Save yourself the pain. Get my [free resource] in bio. Avoid this trap."
```

### Template 3: The Quick Win Tutorial (60s)
```
[0-5s] PROMISE: "Get [specific outcome] in [short time]. Follow these [number] steps."
[5-15s] STEP 1: "[Action]. This takes [timeframe]. Don't skip this."
[15-30s] STEP 2: "[Action]. Pro tip: [insider advice]."
[30-45s] STEP 3: "[Final action]. You'll see [result indicator] immediately."
[45-55s] RECAP: "That's it. [Step 1], [Step 2], [Step 3]. Simple."
[55-60s] CTA: "Want the detailed breakdown? Grab my free [resource] in bio."
```

### Template 4: The Listicle (50s)
```
[0-3s] HOOK: "[Number] things that [impressive result]. Let's go."
[3-12s] THING 1: "[Item]. This alone [specific benefit]."
[12-22s] THING 2: "[Item]. Most people miss this."
[22-32s] THING 3: "[Item]. Game-changer for [outcome]."
[32-42s] THING 4: "[Item]. Do this and [result]."
[42-50s] CTA: "There's a [number+1] bonus in my free guide. Link in bio. Go get it."
```

### Template 5: The Value Bomb (40s)
```
[0-3s] HOOK: "The fastest way to [outcome]? Do this."
[3-10s] CONTEXT: "Most people do [common approach]. It's slow and painful."
[10-25s] THE SECRET: "Instead, [your method]. Here's why it works: [reason]."
[25-35s] EXAMPLE: "I did this for [use case]. [Metric] improved [X]% in [timeframe]."
[35-40s] CTA: "Try it today. Let me know how it goes in the comments."
```

## Voiceover Best Practices

**Pacing:**
- Speak 15-20% faster than normal conversation
- Pause for 0.5s after hook
- Pause for 1s before CTA

**Tone:**
- Conversational, not scripted
- Energy level: 7/10 (not monotone, not shouting)
- Emphasize key numbers and outcomes

**Recording Tips:**
- Record in quiet space (no echo)
- Use phone mic close to mouth (6 inches)
- Record 2-3 takes, pick best energy
- Add captions (80% watch without sound)

## Visual Guidelines

**Text Overlays:**
- Hook text in first frame (bold, large)
- Key numbers/results as on-screen text
- CTA text in last 3 seconds

**B-Roll Cuts:**
- Change visual every 3-5 seconds
- Use transitions (cuts, not effects)
- Show face in hook and CTA (builds trust)

**Engagement Tricks:**
- Point to text overlays
- Use hand gestures for emphasis
- Show results (screenshots, products)
- Before/after comparisons

## Scripting Workflow

1. **Choose framework** (based on content goal)
2. **Write hook first** (test 3-5 variations)
3. **Outline middle** (bullet points, not full script)
4. **Craft CTA** (specific, actionable)
5. **Time it** (read aloud, adjust length)
6. **Record** (multiple takes)
7. **Edit** (cut pauses, add captions, music)
8. **Post and track** (engagement, retention, saves)

## Performance Benchmarks

**Good Script Indicators:**
- 50%+ watch-through rate (UTR)
- 5%+ engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- High saves (people want to reference later)
- Comments asking for more info

**Weak Script Red Flags:**
- Drop-off in first 3 seconds (weak hook)
- Low completion rate (too long, not engaging)
- Views but no engagement (not compelling CTA)

**Iterate Based on Data:**
- Hook weak? Test new first 3s
- Mid-video drop? Tighten middle section
- Low CTA action? Make offer clearer/more valuable
