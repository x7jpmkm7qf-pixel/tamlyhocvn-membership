# Content Calendar System

## Content Pillar System (4-5 Rotating Pillars)

### Pillar 1: Educational
**Purpose:** Teach audience actionable skills/knowledge
**Formats:** How-to posts, tutorials, tips, explainers
**Example Topics:**
- "5 steps to [achieve outcome]"
- "How to [solve problem] in [timeframe]"
- "Beginner's guide to [topic]"
**Frequency:** 40% of content

### Pillar 2: Entertaining
**Purpose:** Build likability, shareability, virality
**Formats:** Memes, relatable stories, humor, trends
**Example Topics:**
- "POV: When you [relatable situation]"
- Trending audio/challenge participation
- "That moment when [funny scenario]"
**Frequency:** 25% of content

### Pillar 3: Promotional
**Purpose:** Drive sales, conversions, leads
**Formats:** Product showcases, testimonials, offers, CTAs
**Example Topics:**
- "New [product/service] available now"
- Customer success story
- Limited-time offer announcement
**Frequency:** 15% of content

### Pillar 4: Personal/Behind-the-Scenes
**Purpose:** Build trust, relatability, connection
**Formats:** Day-in-life, workspace, process, journey
**Example Topics:**
- "How I built [business/product]"
- Workspace tour
- Failures and lessons learned
**Frequency:** 15% of content

### Pillar 5: Engagement
**Purpose:** Increase comments, shares, community building
**Formats:** Questions, polls, challenges, UGC requests
**Example Topics:**
- "Drop a [emoji] if you agree"
- "What's your biggest [challenge]?"
- "Tag someone who needs this"
**Frequency:** 5% of content

## Platform-Specific Posting Schedule

### Facebook (Vietnam Best Times)
**Frequency:** 1-2 posts/day
**Best Times:**
- Morning: 7-9 AM (commute time)
- Lunch: 12-1 PM (break time)
- Evening: 7-9 PM (after work)
**Content Types:** Longer text, link posts, video, live streams
**Optimal Days:** Tuesday-Thursday (highest engagement)

### Instagram
**Frequency:** 1 feed post + 3-5 stories/day
**Best Times:**
- Morning: 8-10 AM
- Lunch: 12-2 PM
- Evening: 7-9 PM
**Feed Content:** High-quality visuals, carousels, Reels
**Stories:** Behind-scenes, polls, quick tips, product teasers
**Optimal Days:** Wednesday-Friday

### TikTok
**Frequency:** 1-3 videos/day
**Best Times:**
- Morning: 6-8 AM (before work)
- Lunch: 12-1 PM
- Evening: 7-11 PM (peak time)
**Content Types:** Trends, tutorials, entertainment, challenges
**Optimal Days:** Thursday-Sunday (weekend spillover)

### YouTube
**Frequency:** 1-2 videos/week
**Best Upload Times:**
- Thursday-Saturday: 2-4 PM (pre-weekend)
**Content Types:** Long-form tutorials, vlogs, deep-dives
**Shorts:** 3-5 per week (repurposed TikTok/Reels)

## Content Types Rotation

**Prevent Format Fatigue:**
- Week 1: Heavy video (Reels, TikTok, YouTube)
- Week 2: Heavy carousels/images (IG, FB)
- Week 3: Mixed (balance video and static)
- Week 4: Interactive (polls, Q&A, live streams)

**Monthly Mix Target:**
- 40% Video (Reels, TikTok, YouTube)
- 30% Images/Carousels
- 20% Text posts
- 10% Interactive (polls, questions, live)

## Batch Creation Workflow

### Monday: Research & Planning (2 hours)
- Review analytics from previous week
- Identify trending topics/sounds
- Outline content ideas for the week
- Assign to content pillars

### Tuesday-Wednesday: Creation (4-6 hours)
- Batch record all videos for the week
- Design graphics/carousels
- Write captions and scripts
- Edit videos (basic cuts, captions, music)

### Thursday: Scheduling (1-2 hours)
- Upload to scheduling tools (Meta Business Suite, Later, Buffer)
- Set optimal posting times
- Add hashtags and tags
- Review for quality/errors

### Friday-Sunday: Engagement & Real-Time (30 min/day)
- Respond to comments/DMs
- Post stories/live content
- Monitor performance
- Adjust upcoming content if needed

## Content Recycling Strategy

**1 Long-Form → 5+ Pieces:**

**Source:** 10-minute YouTube video "How to Start an Online Business"

**Repurposed Content:**
1. TikTok: 60-second highlight of Step 1
2. Instagram Reel: 90-second of Steps 2-3 combined
3. Facebook: Carousel summarizing all 5 steps
4. Instagram Story: 5-slide breakdown (1 step per slide)
5. Blog post: Full transcript + extra details
6. Email newsletter: Key takeaways + video link
7. Quote graphics: Pull 3-5 key quotes from video

**Recycling Timeline:**
- Week 1: Publish YouTube video
- Week 2: Share TikTok + Reel versions
- Week 3: Post carousel + stories
- Week 4: Send email + blog post
- Month 2-3: Reshare top performers with new captions

## Performance Tracking Columns

| Content | Platform | Date | Pillar | Reach | Impressions | Engagement Rate | Saves | Shares | Clicks | Notes |
|---------|----------|------|--------|-------|-------------|-----------------|-------|--------|--------|-------|
| "How to X" Reel | IG | 2026-02-10 | Educational | 5,200 | 8,100 | 6.5% | 120 | 45 | 30 | Strong hook |
| Product launch | FB | 2026-02-11 | Promotional | 1,800 | 2,200 | 3.2% | 10 | 8 | 50 | Good CTA |

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- **Reach:** Unique accounts reached
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Saves + Shares) / Reach × 100
- **Saves:** High-value metric (algorithm boost)
- **Shares:** Virality indicator
- **Clicks:** For promotional content
- **Watch Time:** Video content (aim for 50%+ completion)

**Monthly Review:**
- Top 5 performing posts (by engagement rate)
- Bottom 5 posts (analyze why)
- Best pillar (double down)
- Best format (video vs carousel vs text)
- Best posting times (refine schedule)
