# Social Media KPI Framework

## Overview

This framework defines essential metrics for measuring social media performance. Use these KPIs to evaluate content effectiveness, audience engagement, and platform growth.

## Core Social Media KPIs

### Reach and Awareness Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Industry Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| **Reach** | Unique accounts who saw content | Platform-dependent | Measure audience exposure |
| **Impressions** | Total times content was displayed | 2-3x reach | Track content visibility |
| **Organic Reach Rate** | (Organic Reach / Followers) × 100 | 2-5% Facebook, 10-20% Instagram | Evaluate content distribution |
| **Follower Growth Rate** | ((New Followers - Lost Followers) / Total Followers) × 100 | 2-5% per month | Monitor audience expansion |
| **Share of Voice** | Your mentions / Total industry mentions | Varies by niche | Assess brand visibility vs competitors |

### Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Industry Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| **Engagement Rate (ER)** | (Total Engagements / Reach) × 100 | 1-5% average | Measure content resonance |
| **Engagement Rate by Followers** | (Total Engagements / Followers) × 100 | 1-3% average | Track loyal audience activity |
| **Like Rate** | (Likes / Reach) × 100 | 2-4% | Gauge content appeal |
| **Comment Rate** | (Comments / Reach) × 100 | 0.1-0.5% | Assess conversation generation |
| **Save Rate** | (Saves / Reach) × 100 | 0.5-2% (high-value) | Identify valuable content |
| **Share Rate** | (Shares / Reach) × 100 | 0.5-1% | Measure virality potential |

### Click and Conversion Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Industry Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | (Clicks / Impressions) × 100 | 1-3% social, 4-10% YouTube | Evaluate CTA effectiveness |
| **Link Click Rate** | (Link Clicks / Total Clicks) × 100 | 70-85% | Measure intent-driven actions |
| **Conversion Rate** | (Conversions / Clicks) × 100 | 2-5% average | Track business goal completion |
| **Cost Per Click (CPC)** | Total Ad Spend / Total Clicks | $0.50-$3.00 varies | Assess paid campaign efficiency |
| **Cost Per Engagement** | Total Ad Spend / Total Engagements | $0.05-$0.20 varies | Evaluate engagement cost |

### Video Performance Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Industry Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| **Video Views** | Number of plays (3+ seconds) | Platform-dependent | Track video reach |
| **Average Watch Time** | Total Watch Time / Total Views | 50-60% of duration | Measure content quality |
| **Completion Rate** | (Completed Views / Total Views) × 100 | 50%+ TikTok, 40%+ YouTube | Assess video retention |
| **View-Through Rate (VTR)** | (Views to 95% / Total Views) × 100 | 20-40% | Identify compelling content |
| **Video Engagement Rate** | (Video Engagements / Video Views) × 100 | 3-9% TikTok, 4-8% Reels | Track video interaction |

### Story and Ephemeral Content Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Industry Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------|
| **Story Reach** | Unique accounts who viewed story | 5-10% of followers | Measure story visibility |
| **Story Completion Rate** | (Completed Views / Initial Views) × 100 | 70%+ (good) | Evaluate story engagement |
| **Tap Forward Rate** | (Tap Forwards / Total Taps) × 100 | <10% (ideal) | Identify drop-off points |
| **Exit Rate** | (Exits / Story Views) × 100 | <5% (ideal) | Spot disengaging content |
| **Reply Rate** | (Replies / Story Views) × 100 | 1-3% | Gauge conversation potential |

## Benchmarking Methodology

### Step 1: Identify Your Baseline
- Calculate current performance across all KPIs
- Establish 3-month historical average
- Note seasonal variations and outliers

### Step 2: Set Realistic Targets
- Compare against industry benchmarks
- Account for account size and niche
- Set incremental improvement goals (5-10% quarterly)

### Step 3: Segment Your Analysis
- Break down by content type (video vs image vs carousel)
- Analyze by topic or theme
- Compare organic vs paid performance

### Step 4: Monitor Trends Over Time
- Weekly check-ins for tactical adjustments
- Monthly reviews for strategic planning
- Quarterly benchmarking against competitors

### Step 5: Contextualize Performance
- Consider algorithm updates
- Note external events (holidays, news)
- Account for campaign boosts

## Vanity Metrics vs Actionable Metrics

### Vanity Metrics (Limited Value)

| Metric | Why It's Misleading | What to Use Instead |
|--------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Total Followers | Can be fake or inactive | Follower Growth Rate + Engagement Rate |
| Total Impressions | Doesn't show unique reach | Reach + Frequency Distribution |
| Total Likes | Doesn't indicate value | Save Rate + Comment Sentiment |
| Video Views | Low threshold (3 sec) | Average Watch Time + Completion Rate |
| Page Likes (Facebook) | Doesn't equal engagement | Active Engaged Users |

### Actionable Metrics (High Value)

| Metric | Why It Matters | Business Impact |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| Engagement Rate | Shows content resonance | Builds loyal community |
| Save Rate | Indicates valuable content | Increases content lifespan |
| Share Rate | Measures virality | Expands organic reach |
| Conversion Rate | Tracks business goals | Direct revenue impact |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | Measures efficiency | Optimizes ad spend |

## Advanced KPI Combinations

### Content Quality Score
```
(Engagement Rate × 40) + (Save Rate × 30) + (Share Rate × 30)
```
*Benchmark: 60+ is excellent*

### Audience Growth Efficiency
```
(Follower Growth Rate / Total Content Published) × 100
```
*Benchmark: 1-2% per post is strong*

### Viral Potential Index
```
(Share Rate × 50) + (Completion Rate × 30) + (Comment Rate × 20)
```
*Benchmark: 40+ has viral potential*

### Content ROI
```
(Revenue Generated / Content Production Cost) × 100
```
*Benchmark: 200%+ is profitable*

## KPI Selection Guidelines

### Choose 5-7 Primary KPIs Based On Goals

**Goal: Brand Awareness**
- Reach, Impressions, Share Rate, Follower Growth Rate

**Goal: Community Building**
- Engagement Rate, Comment Rate, Story Replies, DM Rate

**Goal: Traffic Generation**
- CTR, Link Clicks, Bounce Rate (website), Time on Site

**Goal: Sales/Conversions**
- Conversion Rate, Cost Per Acquisition, Revenue Per Follower, ROAS

**Goal: Content Authority**
- Save Rate, Average Watch Time, Completion Rate, Share Rate

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Comparing Apples to Oranges**: Don't compare ER across different platforms
2. **Ignoring Context**: Algorithm changes affect benchmarks
3. **Short-Term Focus**: One viral post doesn't indicate strategy success
4. **Overlooking Audience Quality**: 10K engaged followers > 100K inactive ones
5. **Neglecting Negative Metrics**: High unfollow or exit rates need attention

## Metric Calculation Examples

### Example 1: Instagram Post Analysis
- Reach: 5,000 accounts
- Likes: 300
- Comments: 25
- Saves: 75
- Shares: 20

**Calculations:**
- Engagement Rate: ((300 + 25 + 75 + 20) / 5,000) × 100 = 8.4% (Excellent)
- Save Rate: (75 / 5,000) × 100 = 1.5% (High value content)
- Share Rate: (20 / 5,000) × 100 = 0.4% (Good virality)

### Example 2: TikTok Video Performance
- Views: 50,000
- Likes: 3,500
- Comments: 150
- Shares: 500
- Completion Rate: 65%

**Calculations:**
- Engagement Rate: ((3,500 + 150 + 500) / 50,000) × 100 = 8.3% (Excellent)
- Share Rate: (500 / 50,000) × 100 = 1% (Strong viral potential)
- Viral Potential Index: (1 × 50) + (65 × 30) + (0.3 × 20) = 56 (High)
