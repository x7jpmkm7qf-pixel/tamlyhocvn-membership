# Revenue KPI Framework

## Core Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Total Revenue** | Sum of all completed transactions | Varies by business | Primary business health indicator |
| **Order Count** | Number of successful orders | Growth target: +10-20% MoM | Transaction volume tracking |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | Total Revenue ÷ Order Count | ₫300,000 - ₫2,000,000 (typical) | Per-transaction value indicator |
| **Revenue Growth Rate** | ((Current - Previous) ÷ Previous) × 100 | +15-30% MoM (early stage) | Business expansion velocity |
| **Conversion Rate** | (Orders ÷ Visitors) × 100 | 2-5% (e-commerce standard) | Sales effectiveness |
| **Cart Abandonment Rate** | (Carts Created - Orders) ÷ Carts Created | 60-80% (acceptable) | Checkout friction indicator |
| **Refund Rate** | (Refunded Orders ÷ Total Orders) × 100 | < 5% (healthy) | Product/service satisfaction |
| **Net Revenue** | Total Revenue - Refunds - Chargebacks | Target: 95%+ of gross | Actual retained income |

## Advanced Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** | AOV × Purchase Frequency × Avg. Customer Lifespan | > 3× CAC | Long-term customer value |
| **Revenue per Traffic Source** | Revenue from Source ÷ Total Revenue | Varies by channel | Channel contribution analysis |
| **Repeat Purchase Rate** | (Customers w/ 2+ Orders ÷ Total Customers) × 100 | 20-40% (mature business) | Customer retention indicator |
| **Revenue per Day** | Monthly Revenue ÷ 30 | Smooth trend preferred | Daily performance baseline |

## Profit & Margin Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Purpose |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Gross Margin** | ((Revenue - COGS) ÷ Revenue) × 100 | 60-80% (digital products) | Product profitability |
| **Net Margin** | (Net Profit ÷ Revenue) × 100 | 15-30% (healthy) | Overall business profitability |
| **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)** | Direct costs per unit sold | Minimize while maintaining quality | Unit economics |
| **Marketing Spend %** | (Marketing Cost ÷ Revenue) × 100 | 20-40% (growth phase) | Customer acquisition efficiency |
| **Operating Expense Ratio** | (Operating Costs ÷ Revenue) × 100 | < 30% (efficient) | Business efficiency |

## Conversion Funnel Metrics

| Stage | Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|-------|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Awareness** | Traffic Volume | Total visitors | Consistent growth |
| **Interest** | Engagement Rate | (Engaged Users ÷ Visitors) × 100 | 30-50% |
| **Consideration** | Add-to-Cart Rate | (Add-to-Cart ÷ Visitors) × 100 | 10-20% |
| **Purchase** | Checkout Conversion | (Orders ÷ Add-to-Cart) × 100 | 20-40% |
| **Retention** | Repeat Purchase | See above | 20-40% |

## Vietnamese Market-Specific Considerations

### Common VND Price Points (Psychology)
- ₫99,000 - ₫199,000: Low-ticket impulse purchases
- ₫299,000 - ₫599,000: Mid-range product sweet spot
- ₫799,000 - ₫1,999,000: Premium product range
- ₫2,000,000+: High-ticket coaching/courses

### Bundle Pricing Effectiveness
- 2-product bundle: 15-20% discount drives conversions
- 3+ product bundle: 25-30% discount maximizes perceived value
- Limited-time bundles: +30-50% urgency-driven sales

### Discount Impact on Margins
- 10-15% discount: Minimal margin impact, good for clearance
- 20-30% discount: Acceptable for promotions (maintain 40%+ margin)
- 40%+ discount: Use sparingly (special events only)

### Seasonal Revenue Patterns
- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: Post-Tet dip, budget-conscious buyers
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Steady growth, planning season
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Back-to-school surge (education products)
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Holiday season peak (+40-60% vs. baseline)

## Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) Benchmarks

| Channel | Minimum ROAS | Good ROAS | Excellent ROAS |
|---------|--------------|-----------|----------------|
| Facebook/Instagram Ads | 2:1 | 4:1 | 6:1+ |
| Google Ads | 3:1 | 5:1 | 8:1+ |
| TikTok Ads | 2:1 | 3.5:1 | 5:1+ |
| Zalo Ads | 2.5:1 | 4:1 | 6:1+ |

## Lead-to-Customer Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Lead-to-Customer Rate** | (New Customers ÷ Leads) × 100 | 10-20% |
| **Cost per Lead (CPL)** | Ad Spend ÷ Leads Generated | ₫10,000 - ₫50,000 |
| **Cost per Acquisition (CAC)** | Ad Spend ÷ New Customers | ₫50,000 - ₫300,000 |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | Customer LTV ÷ CAC | > 3:1 (healthy) |

## Red Flags to Watch

1. **Declining AOV**: May indicate pricing pressure or product mix issues
2. **Rising Refund Rate**: Product quality or expectation mismatch
3. **Stagnant Order Count**: Marketing effectiveness declining
4. **Negative Growth Rate**: Immediate strategic review needed
5. **ROAS < 2:1**: Unprofitable ad campaigns
6. **Margin < 40%**: Unsustainable unit economics
7. **CAC > LTV**: Losing money per customer

## Using This Framework

1. **Select Priority Metrics**: Focus on 5-7 core metrics aligned with business goals
2. **Set Baselines**: Record current performance for each metric
3. **Define Targets**: Establish realistic improvement goals
4. **Track Consistently**: Use same calculation methods weekly/monthly
5. **Analyze Trends**: Look for patterns over 4-8 weeks
6. **Take Action**: Prioritize improvements with highest impact
