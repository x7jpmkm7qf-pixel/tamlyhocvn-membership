# Headline and Hook Patterns

Ultimate collection of high-converting headline templates and scroll-stopping hooks.

## HOW-TO HEADLINES

### Pattern 1: Direct How-To
**Template:** "How to [Desired Outcome] Without [Common Obstacle]"
**Examples:**
- How to Build a 6-Figure Agency Without Cold Calling
- How to Lose 20 Pounds Without Counting Calories
- How to Get 1000 Followers Without Paid Ads

### Pattern 2: How-To + Timeframe
**Template:** "How to [Achieve Result] in [Specific Time] (Even If [Objection])"
**Examples:**
- How to Launch Your Course in 30 Days (Even If You Have No Audience)
- How to Close Your First Client in 7 Days (Even If You're Brand New)

### Pattern 3: How-To + Specificity
**Template:** "How to [Outcome] Using [Specific Method/Number]"
**Examples:**
- How to Generate 50 Leads Per Week Using One LinkedIn Post
- How to 10X Your Sales Using 3 Email Templates

### Pattern 4: How I Did It
**Template:** "How I [Achieved Specific Result] and How You Can Too"
**Examples:**
- How I Built a $500K/Year Business From My Bedroom
- How I Grew from 0 to 100K Followers in 6 Months

### Pattern 5: Step-by-Step Guide
**Template:** "The Step-by-Step Guide to [Desired Outcome] for [Target Audience]"
**Examples:**
- The Step-by-Step Guide to LinkedIn Lead Generation for Coaches
- The Complete Roadmap to $10K Months for Freelancers

## QUESTION HEADLINES

### Pattern 6: Yes/No Challenge
**Template:** "Are You Making These [Number] [Mistake Type] Mistakes?"
**Examples:**
- Are You Making These 7 Fatal Copywriting Mistakes?
- Are You Sabotaging Your Sales With These 5 Pricing Errors?

### Pattern 7: What If Scenario
**Template:** "What If You Could [Desired Outcome] Without [Pain Point]?"
**Examples:**
- What If You Could Fill Your Calendar Without Cold Outreach?
- What If You Could Double Revenue Without Hiring More Staff?

### Pattern 8: Why Question
**Template:** "Why [Target Audience] Are [Doing Something] (And You Should Too)"
**Examples:**
- Why Top Agencies Are Ditching Proposals (And You Should Too)
- Why Smart Founders Charge 3X More Than You

### Pattern 9: Do You...?
**Template:** "Do You [Pain Point]? Here's Why (And the Fix)"
**Examples:**
- Do You Struggle to Close High-Ticket Sales? Here's Why
- Do You Feel Stuck at $5K Months? Here's the Breakthrough

### Pattern 10: When Will You...?
**Template:** "When Will You Finally [Achieve Desired Outcome]?"
**Examples:**
- When Will You Finally Quit Your 9-5?
- When Will You Finally Hit $100K/Year?

## NUMBER/LIST HEADLINES

### Pattern 11: Secrets/Ways
**Template:** "[Number] Secrets to [Outcome] That [Authority Figure] Don't Want You to Know"
**Examples:**
- 7 Secrets to Viral Content That Algorithms Love
- 5 Lead Generation Tactics That Industry Leaders Keep Hidden

### Pattern 12: Mistakes to Avoid
**Template:** "[Number] Mistakes Killing Your [Desired Outcome] (And How to Fix Them)"
**Examples:**
- 9 Mistakes Killing Your Conversion Rate (And How to Fix Them)
- 3 Pricing Mistakes Costing You $50K+/Year

### Pattern 13: Reasons Why
**Template:** "[Number] Reasons Why [Target Audience] Choose [Your Solution]"
**Examples:**
- 12 Reasons Why Fortune 500 Companies Use This Framework
- 5 Reasons Why Agencies Switch to Our Platform

### Pattern 14: Things You Need
**Template:** "[Number] Things You Need to [Achieve Outcome] in [Year/Timeframe]"
**Examples:**
- 10 Things You Need to Scale Past $1M in 2026
- 7 Tools You Need to Automate 80% of Your Marketing

### Pattern 15: Best/Top List
**Template:** "The [Number] Best [Things] for [Target Audience] in [Year]"
**Examples:**
- The 15 Best AI Tools for Content Creators in 2026
- The 8 Most Profitable Niches for New Coaches

## FEAR/URGENCY HEADLINES

### Pattern 21: Deadline Warning
**Template:** "You Have [Timeframe] to [Avoid Negative] or [Face Consequence]"
**Examples:**
- You Have 90 Days to Adapt or Get Left Behind
- You Have Until Friday to Lock In 2025 Pricing

### Pattern 22: What You're Losing
**Template:** "Every Day You [Don't Take Action], You Lose [Specific Amount/Result]"
**Examples:**
- Every Day You Wait, You Lose $500 in Potential Revenue
- Every Week Without This System Costs You 20+ Leads

### Pattern 23: Last Chance
**Template:** "Last Chance to [Benefit] Before [Negative Event]"
**Examples:**
- Last Chance to Join Before We Close Enrollment
- Last Chance to Get Founding Member Pricing

### Pattern 24: Running Out
**Template:** "Only [Number] [Thing] Left for [Target Audience]"
**Examples:**
- Only 7 Spots Left for Q1 Consulting Clients
- Only 24 Hours Left to Access This Training

### Pattern 25: The Cost of Waiting
**Template:** "What [Waiting/Inaction] is Really Costing You"
**Examples:**
- What Doing Your Own Marketing is Really Costing You
- What Staying in Your 9-5 is Really Costing You

## Hook Writing Formula

**Structure:** [Pattern Interrupt] + [Curiosity Gap] + [Promise]

**Example:**
- Pattern Interrupt: "I almost quit in 2024."
- Curiosity Gap: "Then I found this loophole."
- Promise: "Now I work 20 hours/week and earn 6 figures. Here's how:"

## A/B Testing Headlines

Test these variables:
1. Specificity (vague vs precise numbers)
2. Timeframe (with vs without)
3. Negative vs positive framing
4. Question vs statement
5. Personal vs impersonal

Track: CTR, engagement rate, conversion rate
