# Persuasion Formulas Library

Complete collection of proven copywriting frameworks for high-converting content.

## 1. Problem-Amplify-Resolve (PAR)

**When to use:** Landing pages, sales emails, VSLs when audience is problem-aware

**Structure:**
1. **Problem** - State the pain point clearly
2. **Amplify** - Intensify the consequences of inaction
3. **Resolve** - Present your solution as the answer

**Example:**
- Problem: "Struggling to close high-ticket sales?"
- Amplify: "Every missed sale costs you $5K+. That's $20K/month walking away."
- Resolve: "Our framework converts 30% of discovery calls into signed contracts."

---

## 2. Transformation Arc

**When to use:** Case studies, testimonials, story-driven sales pages

**Structure:**
1. **Before** - Paint the painful starting point
2. **After** - Show the desired end state
3. **Bridge** - Your solution as the transformation path

**Example:**
- Before: "I was working 80-hour weeks for $3K/month"
- After: "Now I work 20 hours/week and earn $25K/month"
- Bridge: "This system automated 70% of my workflow"

---

## 3. Attention Cascade

**When to use:** Blog posts, email sequences, multi-stage funnels

**Structure:**
1. **Attention** - Hook with bold claim or question
2. **Interest** - Build relevance with details
3. **Desire** - Show transformation and benefits
4. **Action** - Clear CTA with urgency

**Example:**
- Attention: "What if you never needed cold outreach again?"
- Interest: "Inbound leads fill your calendar automatically"
- Desire: "Imagine 50+ qualified prospects messaging YOU first"
- Action: "Claim your free lead magnet system"

---

## 4. Promise-Vision-Evidence-Nudge

**When to use:** Webinars, long-form sales letters, high-ticket offers

**Structure:**
1. **Promise** - Big, tangible outcome
2. **Vision** - Paint the future reality
3. **Evidence** - Proof, testimonials, case studies
4. **Nudge** - Risk reversal + CTA

**Example:**
- Promise: "Triple your revenue in 90 days"
- Vision: "Picture your bank account with an extra $50K"
- Evidence: "37 clients averaged 287% growth"
- Nudge: "30-day guarantee. Start today."

---

## 5. Feature-Edge-Payoff

**When to use:** Product descriptions, feature launches, B2B sales

**Structure:**
1. **Feature** - What it is
2. **Edge** - Why it matters (competitive advantage)
3. **Payoff** - What customer gains

**Example:**
- Feature: "AI-powered proposal generator"
- Edge: "Creates custom proposals 10x faster than competitors"
- Payoff: "Send 50 proposals weekly instead of 5"

---

## 6. Star-Chain-Hook

**When to use:** Personal brand stories, origin stories, emotional connection

**Structure:**
1. **Star** - Introduce relatable character (often founder)
2. **Chain** - Series of events leading to breakthrough
3. **Hook** - The solution discovered becomes your offer

**Example:**
- Star: "I failed 3 businesses before age 30"
- Chain: "Lost $100K, divorced, sleeping on friends' couches"
- Hook: "Then I discovered one framework that changed everything"

---

## 7. ACCA (Awareness-Comprehension-Conviction-Action)

**When to use:** Educational content, explainer videos, onboarding sequences

**Structure:**
1. **Awareness** - Make them realize the problem exists
2. **Comprehension** - Help them understand the root cause
3. **Conviction** - Prove your solution works
4. **Action** - Simple next step

**Example:**
- Awareness: "Most agencies lose 40% revenue to bad retention"
- Comprehension: "Poor onboarding causes 80% of churn"
- Conviction: "Our system keeps 91% of clients past 12 months"
- Action: "Download the retention playbook"

---

## 8. Storytelling Arc

**When to use:** Brand messaging, founder stories, mission-driven content

**Structure:**
1. **Setup** - Ordinary world, relatable situation
2. **Conflict** - Challenge or obstacle emerges
3. **Struggle** - Failed attempts, lessons learned
4. **Resolution** - Breakthrough discovery
5. **Transformation** - New reality achieved
6. **Invitation** - Join the journey

**Example:**
- Setup: "I was a corporate employee dreaming of freedom"
- Conflict: "My side hustle failed 3 times in 2 years"
- Struggle: "Tried every tactic, burned out, almost quit"
- Resolution: "Found one simple system that worked"
- Transformation: "Now I run 7-figure agency, fully remote"
- Invitation: "I'll show you the exact system"

---

## 9. 1-2-3-4 Formula

**When to use:** Quick wins, tutorial content, process-driven offers

**Structure:**
1. **One Problem** - Single, specific pain point
2. **Two Solutions** - Compare inferior vs superior approach
3. **Three Benefits** - Triple value stack
4. **Four Steps** - Simple action plan

**Example:**
- One Problem: "Lead generation is expensive"
- Two Solutions: "Paid ads ($5K/mo) vs organic system ($0)"
- Three Benefits: "Unlimited leads + zero ad spend + builds authority"
- Four Steps: "1. Setup 2. Automate 3. Scale 4. Profit"

---

## Formula Selection Guide

**High-ticket B2B:** Promise-Vision-Evidence-Nudge, ACCA
**E-commerce/DTC:** Problem-Amplify-Resolve, Feature-Edge-Payoff
**Info products:** Transformation Arc, Storytelling Arc
**Quick conversions:** Attention Cascade, 1-2-3-4
**Launches/webinars:** Star-Chain-Hook, Problem-Amplify-Resolve
**Cold traffic:** Storytelling Arc, Attention Cascade

---

## Mixing Formulas

Combine formulas for longer content:
- **Email sequence:** Start with Attention Cascade → follow with Problem-Amplify-Resolve
- **VSL:** Open with Star-Chain-Hook → transition to Promise-Vision-Evidence-Nudge
- **Landing page:** Headline uses Transformation Arc → body uses Feature-Edge-Payoff

---

## Testing Framework

1. Write 3 versions using different formulas
2. Split test for 1 week minimum
3. Analyze conversion data
4. Double down on winner
5. Test variations of winning formula

**Key metrics:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate (CVR)
- Average order value (AOV)
- Time on page
- Scroll depth
